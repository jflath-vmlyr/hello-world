About The Event

Sunday, September 13th @ 8:30AM

This is a self supported sprint triathlon, duathlon and aquabike event that is completely un-sanctioned and just for fun.
No medals will be given out, but maybe a shirt if there is enough interest.
It's not even really a competition, other than against yourself.

Event starts, ends, and transitions at The Tanner's in Lakewood.
